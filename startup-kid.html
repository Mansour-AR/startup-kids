🚀 A summer business simulation • ages 10–16

Startup Kid

It's the first day of summer break and you have $50 of birthday and allowance money. Over 8 weeks you'll start a tiny business, set your prices, get customers, and handle whatever the summer throws at you. Can you turn $50 into a profit — and a few new skills?

👋 Welcome, future founder!

Ever dreamed of running your own business? This is your chance to try it — no risk, all fun. You'll be a young entrepreneur on summer break with one simple mission: take a small bit of money and grow it into something bigger by being smart, creative, and brave.

Here's how it works:

  • 🗓️ Play 8 weeks. Each week you make a few choices, then watch how your business does.
  • 🤔 Every choice matters. Your price, marketing, spending, and energy all change what happens — there's no single "right" answer.
  • 🎲 Expect surprises. Rain, rivals, rave reviews, and lucky breaks can hit any week, just like in real life.
  • 📈 Level up 4 real skills: Pricing, Marketing, Customer Service, and Money Management.
  • 💡 You can't really lose. Even a tough week teaches you something — that's how every entrepreneur starts.
